Sam Le Noel

Sam Le Noel is a 19-year-old racing driver with a passion for motorsport and a mission to prove that success in racing isn’t defined by background or financial resources. Without coming from a motorsport background or having support in this field, Sam has built his journey on long nights, perseverance, and pure love for the sport. With his dream to compete in CTFROT, Sam is focused on developing his skills, building experience, and most importantly inspiring others to chase their dreams — no matter where they start.
